About HOME

Support that is personal, professional, and dependable

Helping Others Means Everything (HOME), LLC is dedicated to providing compassionate, high-quality services that empower individuals and strengthen families. We believe support should feel respectful, consistent, and tailored to each person’s needs.

Our mission

To provide person-centered support that encourages independence, safety, confidence, and community involvement.

Our vision

To be a trusted provider known for compassionate care, reliable staffing, and meaningful outcomes.

Our Services

Services designed around real-life needs

We provide practical, compassionate support that helps individuals build daily living skills, strengthen independence, and stay engaged with their families and communities.

Community Living Supports

Hands-on guidance to build daily living skills, social skills, and greater independence.

Respite Services

Reliable relief for caregivers while loved ones continue to receive supportive care.

Life Skills Support

Help with routines, organization, hygiene, meal preparation, and essential living tasks.

Community Integration

Support that encourages participation, connection, and meaningful community involvement.
