Who we are and why families trust us

Helping Others Means Everything (HOME), LLC is dedicated to delivering compassionate, dependable, and high-quality support services that empower individuals and strengthen families. Our mission is to provide care with professionalism, dignity, and heart.

Our mission

To support individuals and families through person-centered services that promote independence, safety, confidence, and meaningful community involvement.

Our vision

To be a trusted provider known for reliable staffing, compassionate care, and positive outcomes for the people and communities we serve.

Page 2 • Services

Support designed around real-life needs

We provide supportive services that promote independence, safety, community inclusion, and quality of life for individuals and families.

Community Living Supports

Guidance and hands-on support to build daily living skills, social skills, community access, and greater independence.

Respite Services

Reliable relief for caregivers while ensuring loved ones continue to receive quality care and support.

Life Skills Support

Assistance with routines, hygiene, organization, meal preparation, budgeting, and other essential living tasks.

Community Integration

Encouraging participation in community activities, positive relationships, and meaningful engagement beyond the home.
